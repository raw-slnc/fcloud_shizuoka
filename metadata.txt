about=Displays forest information from the Shizuoka Prefecture Forest Cloud public system, linked to GeoPackage data created by Shinrinbo Code Converter. Provides a dock panel for browsing forest stand attributes, protective forest records, and forest management data. This plugin is specific to Shizuoka Prefecture. Requires a .gpkg or .shp layer containing a KEY1 field loaded in the QGIS project. The displayed information (protective forest ledger, Mori no Chikara reforestation project, forest management plans, forest land development, etc.) is sourced from open data published by Shizuoka Prefecture and the Shizuoka Prefecture Forest Cloud, retrieved, processed, and formatted for easy reference in QGIS. / Support: https://paypal.me/rawslnc
